About Us

You Matter is an emotional health service and online therapy provider.
when someone wants to talk about whatever is on their mind can quickly reach out to a listener through our network.
We have many listeners who come from all walks of life and have experiences and are waiting for you.
Unlike talking to family or friends, You Matter listeners don't judge or say what to do. Our listeners just listen and understand. They give you the space you need to help you clear your head.
